Membership is available at two levels:

Full Membership: Open to non-profit primary health care agencies and affords access to all HCCN services and resources. Full Members have representation on the Board of Directors.

Affiliate Membership: No representation on the board of directors. Some Affiliate members house their own IT infrastructure and others access CHAN systems.
