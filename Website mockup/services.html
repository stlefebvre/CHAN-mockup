CHAN's main focus has been Health Information Technologies. Information systems and technology are central tools to the success of the HCCN and hte individual health centers. CHAN hosts fully implemented GE Centricity Electronic Medical Record and Practice Management business systems for CHAN members in our central office.

Centricity Physician office

EMR
Centricity EMR is an electronic medical record system that enables physicians and clinical staff to document patient encounters, streamline clinical workflow, and securely exchange clinical data.

  • Lab & Hospital Documentation Import

  • Document Scanning
